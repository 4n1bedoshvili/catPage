Gemini - The Communicator





Personality Traits:

Gemini, represented by the Twins, embodies duality and adaptability. This Air sign, ruled by Mercury, the planet of communication, exhibits a sharp, quick-witted nature that thrives on information and interaction. Geminis are known for their versatile and intellectual minds, often displaying an insatiable curiosity about the world around them. They are excellent communicators, able to articulate their thoughts and ideas clearly and engagingly. With a natural charm and sociable demeanor, Geminis are adept at navigating various social situations, making new connections with ease.

Likes:

Gemini values freedom and flexibility, seeking relationships and friendships that respect their need for independence and intellectual stimulation.
